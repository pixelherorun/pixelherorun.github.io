About the Game

WHAT IS
DUNGEON RUN?

Pixel Hero: Dungeon Run reimagines dungeon crawling as a fast-paced action platformer — replacing turn-based combat with real-time movement, combat, and precision platforming.

Explore 8 unique dungeon levels packed with traps, secrets, enemies, and treasure. Choose from four distinct classes, adapt your strategy, and discover powerful gear as you descend deeper.

Currently in Beta — the core game is fully playable with more levels on the way. Share your feedback through our in-game review system and help shape the future of Dungeon Run. Face challenging boss fights that demand skill and quick reflexes, with varied builds and platforming-focused gameplay keeping every run fresh.
